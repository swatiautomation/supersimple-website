About Me

The story behind the lens

Hi, I'm Prateek 👋

I'm a passionate photographer based in Canberra, Australia, who believes that every moment deserves to be preserved with beauty and authenticity. My journey started with a small camera and a big dream — and today I'm fortunate to do what I love every single day.

I specialise in wedding photography, portrait sessions, maternity shoots, birthday parties and events across Canberra and the wider ACT region. My style blends natural light, candid emotion and artistic composition to create images that feel both timeless and deeply personal.

Whether it's the joy of a Canberra wedding, the laughter at a birthday, or the quiet intimacy of a family portrait — I aim to freeze those emotions in photographs you'll treasure for a lifetime.
